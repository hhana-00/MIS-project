# MIS-project
# Turtle Smart Mobility Platform — MIS Case Study

A full Management Information Systems (MIS) analysis and business process documentation for **Turtle**, a fictional IoT-enabled micro-mobility startup (scooters & bikes) operating in Egypt.

## Project Overview

This project applies core MIS and business strategy frameworks to model, analyse, and digitally transform a smart mobility company. It covers everything from external environment analysis to business process modelling and digital strategy remodelling using Supply Chain 4.0.

## Frameworks Applied

**PESTLE Analysis**
External environment scan across Political, Economic, Social, Technological, Legal, and Environmental factors affecting Turtle's operations in Egypt.

**SWOT Analysis**
Internal and external factor assessment — covering Turtle's IoT-enabled fleet and eco-positioning as strengths, alongside competitive threats from existing micro-mobility players.

**Business Process Model (BPM)**
Cross-functional swimlane diagram modelling Turtle's end-to-end operations across 5 departments:
- Procurement & Supply Chain
- Legal & Compliance
- Operations & Fleet Management
- IT & Digital Platform
- Finance & Customer Operations

**Balanced Scorecard (BSC)**
Strategic KPI framework across four perspectives — Financial, Customer, Internal Process, and Learning & Growth — linking Turtle's strategy to measurable execution targets.

**Supply Chain 4.0 Remodelling**
Digital transformation of Turtle's organisational supply chain using IoT visibility, predictive maintenance, real-time cross-functional coordination, and automated decision intelligence.
